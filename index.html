PlayStation History

Sony first released the PlayStation on December 3, 1994. The history of PlayStation has expanded ever since the beginning of the first consoles era, most recently with the release of the PlayStation 5. Bringing many people years of enjoyment and fun, Sony has delivered to make memorable experiences with every new generation it has provided to the gaming market. Each console has brought a distinct new benchmark to modern gaming, always improving upon the last of its iterations.
